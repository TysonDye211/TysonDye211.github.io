about 211 electric

  1. Certified Master Electrician in the State of Idaho
  2. Associates degree in Electrical Automation and Instrumentation from Idaho State University
  3. Certified Level 1 Thermography Inspector
  4. Operator/Chemist for 550 megawatt Combined Cycle Power Plant for Exelon Generation
  5. Controls Designer for Power Engineers
  6. Generation Specialist for 101 megawatt Hydroelectric Power Plant in Boise Idaho


Tyson began his electrical career as an Apprentice at Eastern Idaho Technical College. As an Apprentice, he learned the basics of electricity and became skilled in residential electrical installations of re-model and new construction homes. After four years of education he qualified as a Journeyman Electrician where he serviced electrical systems for commercial property managers. While studying at ISU he became trained in electrical automation and control wiring of automated systems for Industrial applications. As he worked in the Power Generation industry, Tyson performed maintenance on High voltage electrical systems and automated interfaces. He is also experienced in the use of Infrared Thermal Imaging cameras to detect signs of electrical failure. In 2016 he certified as a Master Electrician with the State of Idaho. Tyson has a broad range of electrical experience which results now in a robust understanding of the National Electrical Code.

211 Electric was established in 2016 to meet the needs of Commercial, Industrial, and Residential clients in the state of Idaho. Through collective work experience and education, 211 Electric has the expertise to meet all your Electrical needs.
